Bacharuddin Jusuf Habibie 3rd President of Indonesia

Prof Dr.Ing. H. Bacharuddin Jusuf Habibie known as B.J. Habibie was born in Pare-pare, South Sulawesi, 25 June 1936 - died in Jakarta, 11 September 2019. B.J. Habibie is the 3rd President of the Republic of Indonesia. Habibie is the President of Indonesia who has served for the shortest time.

Habibie is known as a professor in aviation technology. In addition, the love story between Habibie and his wife Ainun is very well known in Indonesia, until it was turned into a film.

Studies

The educational history of B.J. Habibie is as follows:

  • 1954 to 1960 - Mechanical engineering at the Bandung Institute of Technology, Indonesia.
  • 1965 - Aviation engineering specializes in aircraft construction at RWTH Aachen, Germany.

Careers

The work history of B.J. Habibie is as follows:

  • Before 1973 - Messerschmitt-Bolkow-Blohm, an airline in Hamburg, Germany.
  • 1978 to 1998 - Indonesian State Minister for Research and Technology.
  • March 1998 to May 1998 - Vice President of Indonesia.
  • May 1998 to October 1999 - President of Indonesia.

Awards

Several awards that have been won by B.J. Habibie like:

  • Honorary Member of The Japanese Academy of Engineering, Japan.
  • Honorary Member of The National Academy of Engineering, USA.
  • Honorary Member of American Institut of Aeronautics and Astronautics, USA.
