popularity survey of crochet products

Knitting and crocheting is very similar. The basic differences is knitting using two needle and crochetting using one hook. Crocheting is a form of art using a hook and yarn. Crochet products consist of decorative products and wearable products. Decorative products such as table mats, pot holds, mandalas, and amigurumi. While wearable products such as bags, wallets, pouches, socks, and baby shoes. This survey aims to determine the popularity and preferences of crochet products against the age of the respondents. The results of this survey will be used as feedback to support and develop product quality.
